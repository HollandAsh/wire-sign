Gallien-Luft
Bakery and Gift Shop

In 2009, sisters Loren Gallien and Julie Luft decided to leave their medical jobs behind, open a bakery featuring their grandmother's recipes to recreate the tastes they loved from childhood. Three years later, the sisters added a gift shop. This gave Julie a chance to be a "micro" art curator; she loves to select the art prints, teacups, coffee mugs offered in the shop. Please stop by for a visit, and be sure to try Grandma Connie's Lemon and Raspberry macaroons.

We specialize in cookies, cheesecakes, and cupcakes.

Prints of the artwork on display throughout the building are available for sale in our Gift Shop

We also carry a selection of tea cups and coffee mugs
