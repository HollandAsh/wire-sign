What is BMI?

Body Mass Index or BMI* is a way of using your height and body mass (weight) to classify you as either: underweight, of normal weight, overweight, or obese.

Please note: BMI is a vague measurement of an individual's body fat. BMI doesn't differentiate between a person with high muscle content and a person with high fat content. If the two people have the same height and weight BMI evaluates both as the same. Meaning that, a muscular person could possibly end up in the obese category.
